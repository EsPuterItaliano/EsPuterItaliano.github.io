ABOUT US

"Hello everyone! We are a dynamic group of high school students from SMA Pangudi Luhur St.Yosef Surakarta, collaborating on an exciting entrepreneurial project. Our team consists of Margareta, Tabita, Odilia, Gabriel, Kathryn, and Yogi, each bringing a unique set of skills and ideas to the table.

Our project revolves around the world of entrepreneurship, focusing on a delightful product – Italiano, a branded frozen yogurt product known as 'es puter yogurt.' Inspired by Margareta's name, Italiano reflects our commitment to quality and creativity.

As a team, we are passionate about creating a product that not only satisfies taste buds but also embodies innovation and a touch of Italian flair. Stay tuned as we embark on this entrepreneurial journey, aiming to bring a delicious and distinctive frozen yogurt experience to our community."
